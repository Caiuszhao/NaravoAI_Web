Design a mobile-first single-page website for an AI interactive narrative entertainment product.
The page is made for potential investors to preview a live interactive content demo on a phone, not for mass-market signup.
It should feel like a mix of TikTok-style vertical immersive video, cinematic sci-fi storytelling, and a premium AI product landing page.

Overall goal:
The website should let an investor immediately understand three things:

this is a new form of interactive content, not a normal short drama and not a normal mini-game
the interaction feels fresh, immersive, and emotionally engaging
the project has clear strategic differentiation and platform potential

Style direction:
dark premium interface, cinematic, futuristic, elegant, minimal but high-impact
mobile UI first, optimized for iPhone screen
visual references: immersive AI-native interactive entertainment websites like Yoroll and Roto AI
strong sense of atmosphere, tension, and emotional pull
use layered depth, soft glow, subtle glassmorphism, clean typography, large cards, rounded corners
avoid corporate SaaS style, avoid generic startup landing page feel
this should feel like a next-generation entertainment product demo

Canvas / frame:
design for mobile first, around 390px width
single-page vertical scroll
everything should look great on a phone screen held in one hand

Page structure:

1. Hero section / opening hook
Full-screen mobile hero with a large vertical-video inspired composition
headline should communicate:
Playable stories, not passive videos
subheadline should communicate:
AI-powered interactive narrative where users influence tension, emotion, and outcomes through actions, dialogue, and performance
include one premium CTA button: Try the Live Demo
include one secondary text link: See Why This Is Different

2. Interactive demo preview section
This is the core of the page
show a big phone mockup or embedded vertical-player style module
inside it, simulate a short immersive story scene, like:

escaping a dangerous world
saving someone under pressure
emotionally responding to a character
performing an action to change the outcome

The interaction should not feel like multiple-choice buttons.
It should feel like behavior-based interaction, such as:

rapid tapping
swipe to unlock or cut through obstacle
long press to stabilize a system
short voice / text response to influence a character
motion / rhythm / timing-based input

Show clear UI hints such as:

“Tap fast to break the gate”
“Hold to stabilize power”
“Say something to calm her down”
“Swipe before time runs out”

Make this section feel extremely immersive, like the investor can imagine it actually running on a phone.
Strong sense of urgency, emotional tension, and immediate consequence.

3. Why this feels new
a concise section with 3 premium cards
explain the key product differentiation:

Card 1: Interaction is part of the story
Users do not just choose options, they perform actions that change pacing, trust, and outcomes

Card 2: AI characters extend beyond the video
Characters are not static roles, they can respond, remember, and continue interaction outside the original scene

Card 3: Stories can become remixable worlds
Each narrative can branch into replay, continuation, and user-created variations

Make these cards visually sharp, premium, and easy to scan on mobile

4. Product thesis / investor-facing positioning
add a section that frames the project as:
A character-driven interactive narrative platform
not a traditional short drama app
not only a chat-based character AI app
but a new content format that combines:

cinematic short-form storytelling
action-based interaction
AI-driven characters
repeatable narrative worlds

This section should feel clear, strategic, and concise
use a clean diagram or layered visual stack

5. User experience flow
show a simple 4-step mobile-friendly visual flow:

Watch a story moment
Act / speak / respond
Trigger emotional or narrative consequence
Unlock continuation, replay, or remix

Make this feel intuitive and productized

6. Why it matters / market potential
short, elegant, investor-friendly section
explain that this format has the strengths of:

stronger immersion than passive short video
lower friction than traditional games
stronger emotional retention through AI characters
better replayability and UGC potential through branching and remix

Keep the language high-level, strategic, and visionary

7. Final CTA / closing section
strong mobile footer section
headline: The next content format is playable
subheadline: Interactive narrative built for the short-video generation
CTA button: Book a Demo

UI / visual details:

use large, phone-native card layouts
elegant dark background with cinematic gradients
subtle neon or glow accents, but not too flashy
soft glass layers and premium shadows
rounded corners, vertical rhythm, clean spacing
minimal but expressive icons
typography should feel premium and modern
prioritize emotional atmosphere over information density
every section should feel easy to consume on a phone

Important constraints:

do not design this as a desktop SaaS homepage
do not use generic marketing sections like pricing tables, client logos, blog cards
do not make it feel like a normal streaming app
do not make interaction feel like a quiz or choose-your-own-adventure menu
focus on immersion, emotional tension, and action-triggered narrative change

Keywords:
mobile-first, vertical video, interactive story, AI characters, cinematic, immersive, behavioral interaction, dark premium, futuristic entertainment, investor demo, next-generation narrative platform
